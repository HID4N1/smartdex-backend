PRICING INFORMATION — SMARTDEX

[OVERVIEW]
This document defines pricing structures, ranges, and rules used by Smartdex for estimating and generating quotes for digital services. Pricing is adapted based on project complexity, client type, and required features.

Currency: MAD (Moroccan Dirham)

---

* Basic websites: 5,000 – 15,000 MAD
* Professional websites: 15,000 – 30,000 MAD

[PRICING MODIFIERS]

Increase price if:

* Multiple user roles (+20% to +40%)
* Real-time features (+20%)
* Third-party integrations (+10% to +30%)
* Custom UI/UX design (+15% to +25%)
* Tight deadlines (+20%)

Decrease price if:

* Template-based solution (-20%)
* Long-term contract (-10% to -25%)
* MVP scope reduction (-15%)

---

[CLIENT SEGMENTATION]

Small businesses:

* Budget-sensitive
* Focus on MVP or basic systems
* Typical range: 5,000 – 20,000 MAD

SMEs:

* Need automation and dashboards
* Typical range: 20,000 – 80,000 MAD

High-value clients / enterprises:

* Require scalability and integrations
* Typical range: 80,000 – 200,000+ MAD

---

[POSITIONING RULES]

* Smartdex does NOT compete on lowest price
* Focus is on value, automation, and long-term ROI
* Prefer building systems over simple websites
* Encourage upselling from websites → automation → full systems

---

[QUOTE GENERATION LOGIC]

When generating a quote:

1. Identify service type (website, app, AI, etc.)
2. Estimate complexity (basic, advanced, complex)
3. Apply relevant base price range
4. Add modifiers based on features
5. Adjust based on client segment
6. Return a price range, not a fixed number

Example:
"Advanced web app with automation for SME"
→ Base: 60,000 MAD
→ + automation (+20%)
→ Final estimate: 70,000 – 85,000 MAD
